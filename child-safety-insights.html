Notes — Definitions & boundaries
4.5 AI Transparency Failures — Distinct from 4.2 and 3.5: covers epistemic opacity about the AI's own nature, limitations, and biases.
7.1 / 7.2 — 7.1 = commercial/financial exploitation; 7.2 = motivational manipulation through design.
8.2 Cognitive Substitution — Distinguish inhibitive substitution (harms development) from capacitative substitution (compensates for special needs).
⚠ 8.5 Sensorimotor & Social Displacement — Boundary: replacement of embodied physical experiences only. Exclude cognitive (→ 8.2) and affective (→ 3.5).
